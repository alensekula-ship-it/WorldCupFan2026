GOOGLE PLAY DATA SAFETY - DRAFT ANSWERS

App: World Cup Fan 2026

Does the app collect or share any of the required user data types?
Recommended answer: No

Data collection:
- The app does not require login.
- The app does not create user accounts.
- The app does not collect name, email, phone number, location, contacts, photos, messages, financial info or health info.
- The app saves selected team, language, theme and prediction scores locally on the device only.

Data sharing:
- The app does not share personal data with third parties.
- Share Prediction uses the Android share sheet only when the user manually chooses to share content.

Security:
- No account system.
- No server sync.
- No cloud backup.
- No personal data sent from the app.

Delete data:
- Users can reset scores in the app.
- Users can clear app data through Android system settings.

If ads, analytics, crash reporting, cloud sync, push notifications or login are added later, these answers must be updated.
