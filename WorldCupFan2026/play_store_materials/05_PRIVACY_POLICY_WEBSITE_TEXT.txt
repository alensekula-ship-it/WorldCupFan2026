Privacy Policy for World Cup Fan 2026

World Cup Fan 2026 is an independent fan-made football tournament companion app.

The app does not require login, account creation or a personal profile.
The app stores selected team, language, theme and prediction scores locally on the user's device.

The app does not collect personal data.
The app does not sell personal data.
The app does not share personal data with third parties.
The app does not provide betting, gambling, live streaming or official tournament media.

Users can reset prediction scores in the app.
Users can also clear app data through Android system settings.

World Cup Fan 2026 is not affiliated with FIFA or any official tournament organizer.
The app does not use official FIFA logos, official crests or player photos.
