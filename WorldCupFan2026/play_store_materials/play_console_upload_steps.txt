GOOGLE PLAY CONSOLE - UPLOAD STEPS

1. Create new app in Google Play Console.
2. App name: World Cup Fan 2026.
3. Default language: English or Croatian.
4. App type: App.
5. Category: Sports.
6. Set app as free or paid.
7. Complete App content:
   - Privacy Policy URL
   - Data Safety
   - Content Rating
   - Target audience
   - Ads declaration
8. Go to Testing -> Internal testing.
9. Create new release.
10. Upload app-release.aab.
11. Add release notes.
12. Save and review.
13. Roll out to internal testers.

Important:
Before Production release, test install from Google Play internal testing link.
