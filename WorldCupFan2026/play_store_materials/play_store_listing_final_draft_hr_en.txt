ENGLISH FULL DESCRIPTION
World Cup Fan 2026 is an independent fan-made tournament companion for football fans.

Pick your national team, enter match scores, calculate group tables, follow the road to the final and share your prediction with friends.

Features:
- 48 teams and 12 groups
- Offline score predictor
- Group standings
- Best third-placed teams
- My Team hub
- Host cities
- Players hub
- Golden Boot and MVP prediction
- Visual bracket
- Share prediction poster
- Dark and light mode
- Multiple languages

Important:
This is an independent fan-made app. It is not affiliated with FIFA. No official FIFA logo, official crests, player photos, live streaming, betting or gambling services are included.
